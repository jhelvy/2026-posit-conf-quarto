Quarto keeps your content correct

Load: you, the agent, and your data. Click 1: you hand the agent a prompt AND your data. Click 2: it generates the output — format’s fine, but the content is ragged and unverified; all you can do is review and re-prompt. Click 3: the .qmd appears; now the agent only writes the .qmd, and your data arrow swings off the agent and onto the .qmd — your content never passes through the model. Click 4: the .qmd renders to the output; the content snaps to exact, still perfectly formatted. Click 5: you edit structure and content directly. One lever became three.
