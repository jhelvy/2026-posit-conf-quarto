The standard workflow

Boxes are already in place. Click 1: you prompt the agent. Click 2: it generates the whole finished output across the gap — content and format come out interleaved, fused, you can’t pull them apart. Click 3: all you can do is review and re-prompt. Your only lever on the output is the agent itself.

Quarto in the middle

Same boxes, locked in place — the Quarto layer drops into the gap. Click 1: the agent writes only the .qmd. Click 2: the .qmd reads the data, and together they render to the output — now a clean blue-over-amber stack instead of an interleaved mush. Click 3 (the payoff): you reach past the agent and edit the structure and the content directly. One lever became three.
